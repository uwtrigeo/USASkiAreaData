Washington State Avalanche Prone Areas
The avalanche prone areas in the boundaries of the six major Washington state ski resort are displayed. The data on this map was created by obtaining digital terrain models (DTM) for each ski area. The DTM for each resort was then analyzed in ArcGIS Pro where the slope and aspect was establshed. Those two layers were then combinded and the North and East facing slopes with a pitch between 30 and 40 degrees were then isolated and applied to this map.

Size, Elevation, and Vertical Drop
The ski resort size, summit elevation, base elevation, and vertical drop for 365 resorts across the USA is displayed on this map.
